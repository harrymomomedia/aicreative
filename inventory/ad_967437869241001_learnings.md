## Captions

- The user rejected large/ugly captions. Use Submagic-like sizing: around 3-4% of frame height, max 2 rows, adaptive shrink for long chunks.
- Do not let `SIGNIFICANT POTENTIAL FINANCIAL COMPENSATION` wrap to 4 rows. Adaptive shrink to 2 rows is required.
- Keep proper nouns visually correct in captions even when audio uses phonetic spelling.

## Do Not Repeat

- Do not assume Seedance's 4s proper-noun success guarantees 14s success; re-test or use the exact corrected spelling that worked.
- Do not use multi-reference Seedance as a storyboard mechanism for multiple B-roll shots; it blends references instead of making reliable cuts.
